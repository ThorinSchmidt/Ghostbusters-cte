India

India. It is a large peninsula on the southern edge of the Asian continent; it was once considered to be the "Crown Jewel of the British Empire." Now thankfully independent once more, India is still a land full of mystery and magic. It was long isolated from the rest of the world, and was(is) home to powerful gods, magicians, and strange beasts. Even today, many "undiscovered" areas still exist, isolated from the Modern World.

Even in Shrewsbury Smith's time however, the "powers" of India were beginning to escape their long exile, making their presence felt in the wider world through the Industrial West's ever-growing sphere of commerce.
